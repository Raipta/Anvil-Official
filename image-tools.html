How to compress an image online

  1. Upload your image using the box under "Compress Image".
  2. Set your target max file size in MB.
  3. Click "Compress Image" — the result downloads automatically, processed entirely on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Will compressing reduce image quality?

Some quality loss is expected with smaller file sizes, but the tool balances size and visual quality automatically.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG and WebP are supported for upload and conversion.

Does Background Remover work on any photo?

It uses a real on-device AI segmentation model, so it works on people, animals, products and most subjects — not just plain backgrounds. Very busy or low-contrast images may still be imperfect around fine details like hair.

Is the AI model uploaded to a server when I remove a background?

No. The AI model downloads once to your browser and all processing happens on your device — your photo is never sent anywhere.
